เกี่ยวกับโครงการ About the Project

This web application was developed as part of the research project titled Effectiveness of the Use of Conversation Analysis (CA)-Informed Closed Captions in Improving Spoken English of Thai EFL Learners. The project builds on the CA-informed Teaching (CA-T) approach, which aims to enhance the English conversation skills of Thai EFL learners.

Funded by the National Science, Research and Innovation Fund (NSRF) and Prince of Songkla University under grant #LIA6701014S, this collaborative initiative brings together the Research Center for Language, Culture, and Human Development in Lower ASEAN at the Faculty of Liberal Arts, Prince of Songkla University (PSU), and the Faculty of Informatics, Burapha University.

Its primary objective is to empower educators to improve Thai EFL learners' English pronunciation through a technology-enhanced prosodic pronunciation assessment system.
